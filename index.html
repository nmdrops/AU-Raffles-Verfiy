🎟️ NM Raffles

Independent Draw Verification

How it works

When a raffle is created, the bot generates a secret seed and publishes its SHA-256 commit hash in the announcement. This proves the outcome was predetermined. After the draw, the seed is revealed. Anyone can paste the values below and verify the result was not manipulated.

🎮 How to Play

1
Buy Credits
Run /buycredits in the Discord server and choose how many credits you want. You'll receive a secure Stripe payment link — 1 credit = $1.00 AUD. Credits are added to your account automatically once payment is confirmed.
2
Find an Active Raffle
Head to #active-raffles. Each raffle shows the prize, ticket price in credits, total slots available, max tickets per member, and the draw date and time.
3
React to Enter
Click the 🎟️ reaction on the raffle post. The bot will DM you asking how many tickets you'd like. Reply with a number — the bot will confirm your entry and deduct credits from your balance.
4
Wait for the Draw
At the listed draw time the bot automatically selects winners using a provably fair HMAC-SHA256 algorithm. Winners are announced in #winners and DMed directly by the bot.
5
Claim Your Prize
Winners can choose the physical prize or 80% of the estimated market value paid directly to their bank account by an admin. An admin will reach out via DM to arrange delivery or payment.
